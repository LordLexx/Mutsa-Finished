How It Works

To order an item from the United Kingdom, please write us a message below specifying your needs. Also make sure to copy and paste the product link from a UK website such as Amazon UK or eBay UK into our message box below. Then, fill out the required fields so we can process your request accurately. This helps us ensure we get the exact item you want. If you prefer a quicker response, please message us directly on WhatsApp, and our team will assist you right away.

To order anything from Zimbabwe, simply write the name of the item you want in the message box below or send us a message on WhatsApp for a faster response. You can order anything—from snacks and traditional medicine to clothing and everyday essentials like Upfu, Nzungu, Nyimo, Maheu, and more. Whatever you need from Zimbabwe, we’ll make sure to get it for you!
