Who Are We?

We are a dedicated team of Zimbabweans based in the UK, committed to connecting Zimbabweans both at home and abroad with trusted, reliable shopping solutions. Our mission is to make it easier for Zimbabweans in the UK to purchase products from Zimbabwe, and for those in Zimbabwe to access goods from the UK. Whether you're looking for groceries, electronics, clothing, or any other product, we provide a secure, efficient, and fast shipping service that ensures your items arrive safely and on time. With a strong focus on trust and customer satisfaction, we are here to bridge the gap and bring convenience to your shopping experience.


To place an order, simply choose your desired product from any company or vendor — whether it’s from eBay, Amazon, Apple, Samsung, or any other trusted retailer. Once you’ve found what you need, share the product details with us, and we’ll handle the rest. Our efficient process ensures that your items are delivered to you safely and swiftly, typically within a week. With our reliable service, you can shop with confidence knowing that we are committed to bringing you what you need, when you need it.
